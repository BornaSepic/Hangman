About

My journey as a web developer started at the age of 14 when I first came across HTML and CSS while attending a classical gymnasium in Pazin. Unfortunately, at the time, I focused more on my academic life and neglected to hone my web development skills.

Fast forward four years, while attending the Faculty of Civil Engineering in Rijeka I realized where my true passion lies. Within a years time, I was able to quit university and transition into a full-time job as a freelancer.

I'm currently working with several smaller business on various projects, as well as spending a considerable portion of my free time learning new technologies and trying to stay on top of the ever-changing landscape of modern web development.
